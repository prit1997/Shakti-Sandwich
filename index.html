Shakti – The Premium Sandwich Shop

Shakti offers a great variety of lip-smacking and innovative sandwiches that you have never tasted before. The exclusive and unique flavour of each of our sandwiches defines our chef and dedication towards your satiation!

We aim to provide the right food to your hunger pangs in a completely new way. We serve highly nutritious sandwiches of assorted ranges perfectly infused with appetizing ingredients. While you make the bite into the soft, delicious, cheesy pack-ins filled with delicious paneer, you can feel the real taste!

Out of the number of 25-odd varieties of sandwiches, we have a menu that suits your taste. You can find a cheesy and zesty Cheese Chilli Sandwich and Cheese Garlic Sandwich or a traditional and soul-stirring Ahmadabad Touch Sandwich. All our delicious and yummy sandwiches and crispy French-fries are a treat to your taste buds.

We focus on the attractive and tempting presentation of our menus! Our best picks are Tandoor Paneer, Russian, Mexican, Paneer Schezwan, Tangy Touch and Winter Special Sandwiches. To add to your delight, you can savour our scrumptious simple cheesy and tasty sandwiches that exude a heavenly flavour. To complement your hunger pangs, we offer you the French fries, desert sandwiches and bhel.

Feel the flavoursome ingredients that are so well brewed up to serve you the mouth-watering sandwiches while you gorge on your desired dish.
